Cardiology

Cardiology is the medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disorders related to the cardiovascular system, which includes the heart and blood vessels

Cardiologists, the medical professionals specialized in cardiology, are responsible for evaluating and managing a wide range of heart conditions, such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and congenital heart defects, to ensure the overall cardiovascular health of their patients.

Neurology

Neurology is a branch of medical science and healthcare that focuses on the study, diagnosis, and treatment of disorders and diseases related to the nervous system.

Neurology treatment in medical laboratories (medi labs) primarily involves the diagnosis and assessment of neurological conditions rather than direct treatment. Neurology is a medical specialty that deals with disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Heptalogy

A heptalogy is a literary or storytelling work that consists of seven distinct and interconnected parts, which together form a larger narrative or story arc.

Similar to a trilogy (which consists of three parts) or a tetralogy (which consists of four parts), a heptalogy extends the narrative complexity by having seven components. These individual parts may contribute to the overall plot, character development, or themes of the entire work and are usually meant to be read or experienced sequentially to fully understand the overarching story.
